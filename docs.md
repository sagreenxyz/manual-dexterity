# Documentation

Welcome to the Manual Dexterity documentation site.

## Available Resources

This documentation site contains comprehensive nursing education materials from Elsevier, including:

### Elsevier Textbooks

- **Saunders Comprehensive Review for the NCLEX-RN Exam, 9th Edition**
  - 19 units covering all aspects of NCLEX-RN preparation
  - 70 chapters with comprehensive review content

- **Basic Nutrition and Diet Therapy, 16th Edition**
  - 4 parts covering nutrition science and clinical applications
  - 23 chapters on nutritional principles and practice

- **Pharmacology and the Nursing Process, 10th Edition**
  - 10 parts covering drug classifications and nursing applications
  - 58 chapters on pharmacological principles and patient care

- **Foundations of Nursing, 11th Edition**
  - 7 units covering nursing fundamentals and practice
  - 50 chapters on essential nursing concepts and skills
